How does the "Final Exam Grade Calculator" work?

In most Universities/Institutions in the world, final grades are calculated based on exams, quizzes, homework assignments, attendance, and projects. Each of these categories are assigned a weight. I.e. projects are worth 20%, exams are worth 16%, etc. With these grade weights, along with your current and desired grade, we can calculate the required grade to hit your desired grade based on whichever category you choose.
