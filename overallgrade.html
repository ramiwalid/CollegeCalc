How does the "Projected Grade Calculator" work?

This tool receives several inputs consisting of your current grade in a class, the grade you received on an assignment, and the weight that assignment has on your grade; and outputs for you what your final grade will be AFTER that assignment is officially graded (your projected grade). This tool is very useful if you have received a grade and want to see how it'll affect your grade, or, if you want to test what your final grade will be if you get a certain score on an assignment.
